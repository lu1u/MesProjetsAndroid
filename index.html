Frequent launcher widget

A widget that let you launch your most frequent applications, without the need to configure anything. This program uses a service that regularly checks the foreground activity. Unfortunately, you cannot intercept the launch of an application for security reasons.

Comes in two flavours:

Listview

Stackview
